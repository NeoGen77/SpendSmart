# SpendSmart

A powerful mobile application for managing personal finances with intelligent spending analytics and forecasting. SpendSmart helps you take control of your money by tracking expenses, planning budgets, and gaining insights through statistical analysis.

## ✨ Features

- 💰 **Expense Tracking**: Easily log and categorize your spending
- 📊 **Budget Planning**: Set budgets and receive alerts when approaching limits
- 📈 **Statistical Analysis**: Visual charts and graphs of your spending patterns
- 🔮 **Spending Projections**: AI-powered forecasts for monthly and yearly spending trends
- 💡 **Smart Insights**: Identify spending habits and opportunities to save
- 🔐 **Secure**: Your financial data stays private and secure
- 📱 **Mobile-First**: Intuitive design optimized for on-the-go money management

## 📖 Usage

### Basic Workflow

1. **Create an Account**: Sign up with your email and secure password
2. **Set Your Budget**: Define monthly budgets for different spending categories
3. **Log Expenses**: Add expenses as you spend or batch-log at the end of the day
4. **View Analytics**: Check dashboards for spending summaries and trends
5. **Review Projections**: See forecasted spending based on historical data
